​Challenge No. 3
​Title: Omnichannel AI Customer Engagement Agent — وكيل التفاعل الذكي متعدد القنوات مع المتعاملين

​Problem Statement:

​PROBLEM: MOEI engages with customers across multiple channels — WhatsApp, the contact center (voice), and the website/mobile application — but each channel currently operates in isolation, with limited AI capabilities, manual dependencies, and fragmented operational visibility. This results in delayed responses, inconsistent service quality across channels, repeated customer effort (customers re-explaining the same issue on each channel), limited personalization, and reactive rather than proactive engagement. Leadership lacks a unified, real-time view of customer experience and operational performance across all touchpoints.

CHALLENGE: Build an Agentic AI system that provides a unified, intelligent customer engagement layer across all three channels (WhatsApp, Voice Contact Center, and Website & Mobile Application):

A) Conversational Intelligence (cross-channel):
• Natural Language Understanding in Arabic and English with consistent intent recognition, shared knowledge base, and persistent customer context across channels.
• Generative AI–powered responses for inquiries, complaints, suggestions, and appreciations — with seamless handover between channels (e.g., start a request on WhatsApp, complete on the mobile app).
• Real-time voice tone and sentiment analysis on calls (stress, anger, satisfaction) feeding the same unified customer profile used in chat and the app.

B) Operational Execution:
• Automated case creation, tracking, routing, and resolution integrated with CRM and backend government services.
• Instant updates, smart notifications, and proactive follow-ups via the customer’s preferred channel.
• Personalized service guidance, next-best-action recommendations, and intelligent self-service based on customer history and behavior.
• AI-powered smart search across services, policies, FAQs, and procedures — available consistently on web, mobile, and chat.

C) Agent & Leadership Enablement:
• AI co-pilot for human contact-center agents (next-best-action, automated knowledge support, decision support, real-time coaching).
• Unified executive dashboard with real-time KPIs, sentiment trends, channel-level performance, escalation risks, and workforce productivity.
• Predictive analytics for call/chat volume forecasting, demand patterns, escalation risks, and proactive workforce planning.

SUCCESS LOOKS LIKE: A working prototype demonstrating a single, unified AI customer engagement agent operating across WhatsApp, the voice contact center, and the website/mobile application — with measurable improvements in time-to-resolution, first-contact resolution rate, customer effort score, CSAT, and self-service deflection. Leadership has one real-time dashboard providing full omnichannel visibility, and human agents have a working AI co-pilot.

DATASET PROVIDED: CRM data, customer profiles, service request history, chatbot conversations, WhatsApp interaction logs, NICE contact center data and call recordings, operational KPIs, queue and handling-time metrics, agent activity logs, website analytics, mobile application usage data, search logs, FAQs, service catalogues, and customer feedback data.
